90's Jungle

About

Mark fisher wrote extensively on music and music culture in numerous books, magazine pieces and essays and on his K-Punk Blog (which can be found ), about a vast range of genres and movements- however one genre stands out above all, a fondness for which always remained close to Mark Fisher's heart- that of 90's Jungle.

"Our sense of the futuristic now belongs to the past... it has not been updated since the 90's, really. In the 90's with, you know, genres like jungle, they felt radically unprecented... they felt like nothing heard before nor could you have heard it before.. there was a feeling of the future rushing in towards us..." -Mark Fisher, 2014.
